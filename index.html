How to Load Scripts

  1. Go to the Xeno Executor and download the latest release.
  2. Complete any required ads or steps to unlock the download.
  3. Open the main Xeno .exe application.
  4. Paste your script into Xeno, then press execute.
  5. Once finished, the GUI and other features will appear in Roblox.
